==============
Screen Utility
==============

Launch Screen: screen
Show screen parameters: <CTRL>+<A> then <?>
Detach the screen: <CTRL>+<A> then <d>
Reattach the screen: screen -r <socket>
List available screen sessions: screen -ls
Switch between nested screens: <CTRL>+<A>, then <p> or <n>
Create a new screen: <CTRL>+<A>, then <c.
Kill a screen session: <CTR>+<A>, then <K>
